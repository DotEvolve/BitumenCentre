About Us

Our Mission

To help bring water and food to more families across the world by delivering better and more affordable and quality bitumen to more end-users globally, assisting them to transform and build more bituminous roads and to help build better infrastructure for the world of tomorrow, all this with great enthusiasm and love to what we do.

Company History

Bitumen Centre, having its headquarters in Tanzania, was incorporated in 2005.

Our focus area is provide our customers with quality products related to infrastructure like Bitumen, used for road construction and all other products related to road making. All our products confirm to the quality standard requirements of the Bureau of Standard of respective country.

Our Products

In Bitumen we are mainly dealing with Bitumen 60/70 and Bitumen 80/100 of which we always maintain a stock of 500 M.T each grade for local supply as well as for re-export to other countries. We also supply other grades like Cutback MC30, Emulsion K1-60, SS60, etc which we order specially for our customers against order being shorter shelf life of these products.

Service Quality

We are always striving to improve the quality of roads by supplying quality products. We also intend to supply 2 special types of Bitumen i.e Performance Grade Bitumen also known as PG Grade as well as Polymer Modified Bitumen (PMB). These bitumen increases the life of the road which inturn is beneficial for the public as well as the country.

Due to our quality products our clients have developed trust in us and we shall always continue to supply the best products.
